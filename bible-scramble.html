Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Bible Scramble ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Bible memory game application.

Information We Collect

Bible Scramble is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Storage Data: Your game progress, completed verses, completion times, and favorite verses are stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • No Analytics: We do not track your usage patterns or collect analytics data

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing the Bible memory game functionality
  • Tracking your learning progress
  • Managing your favorite verses
  • Improving your user experience

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not:

  • Upload your data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use cloud storage for your personal information

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local storage encryption where available
  • Secure data handling practices
  • No transmission of personal data over networks

Third-Party Services

Bible Scramble does not integrate with third-party services that collect personal information. The app operates independently without external data collection.

Children's Privacy

Bible Scramble is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from children or adults. All data remains on the user's device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data through the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Reset your progress within the app settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
